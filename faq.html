Cost & Insurance Questions

How much does water damage restoration cost?

Costs vary based on the extent of damage, but typical ranges are:

  • Minor damage (small leak, limited area): $500 - $1,500
  • Moderate damage (burst pipe, several rooms): $2,000 - $7,000
  • Major damage (flooding, structural): $7,000 - $15,000+

We offer free estimates and work with all insurance companies. Most homeowners insurance covers sudden, accidental water damage.

Does homeowners insurance cover water damage?

Usually, but it depends on the cause. Insurance typically covers sudden, accidental water damage like burst pipes or appliance failures. It usually does NOT cover gradual damage from maintenance issues or flooding from outside sources (which requires separate flood insurance).

We work directly with your insurance company and handle all documentation.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates. Our technician will assess the damage, explain the restoration process, and provide a detailed quote. We can often provide a rough estimate over the phone for emergency situations.

The Restoration Process

What happens when I call for water damage restoration?

  1. Immediate response: We answer 24/7 and dispatch a crew immediately
  2. On-site assessment: Under 60-minute arrival in Tampa Bay; we assess damage and begin extraction
  3. Water extraction: Industrial pumps remove standing water
  4. Drying: Commercial dehumidifiers and air movers dry the structure
  5. Restoration: Repairs to return your home to pre-damage condition

Do I need to move out during restoration?

For minor to moderate damage, you can usually stay in your home. For extensive damage or if hazardous materials (like sewage) are involved, temporary relocation may be necessary. We'll advise you based on your specific situation.

Will you work with my insurance company?

Absolutely. We work with ALL insurance companies. We document everything with photos, provide detailed reports, and can bill your insurance directly. Our goal is to make the claims process as smooth as possible for you.

Timing & Response

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We offer 24/7 emergency response with under 60-minute arrival times throughout Tampa Bay including Riverview, Brandon, Wesley Chapel, and surrounding areas. We understand that water damage gets worse every minute.

How long does water damage restoration take?

  • Water extraction: Same day
  • Drying process: 3-5 days typically
  • Minor repairs: 1-3 days
  • Major restoration: 1-3 weeks

We'll give you a specific timeline after assessing your damage.

How quickly does mold grow after water damage?

In Florida's humid climate, mold can begin growing within 24-48 hours of water damage. This is why fast response is critical. Our restoration process includes antimicrobial treatments to prevent mold growth.
